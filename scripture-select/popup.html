Scripture Select

Scripture Select is a simple tool to help you make connections with the scriptures as you visit different webpages.

As you browse, simply simply select plain text (words or phrases) with your cursor, and click the extension icon, and you will be shown every occurance of that text in the Bible, English Standard Version. Thank you, and may God give His blessing to the reading of His word.
